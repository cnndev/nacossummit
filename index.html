The Largest Innovation Summit in Africa.

The National Innovation Summit, organized by the Nigeria Association of Computing Students, is an annual event that brings together students from various ICT disciplines in Nigeria and West Africa to empower them with global software knowledge. The summit aims to facilitate and build skilled capacities for self-reliance, wealth creation, and national security through the application of global best practices in Information and Communications Technology.

The National Innovation Summit is a valuable experience for anyone interested in ICT and seeking to advance their knowledge, skills, and career prospects in this field.
